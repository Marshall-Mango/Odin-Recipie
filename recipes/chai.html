Chai

To make a cup of tea heat tea leaves in milk and then add sugar to sweeten it

    Ingredients required for making Chai
  • Tea leaves
  • Milk
  • Sugar
    Steps for making Chai
  1. Heat milk and then add the tea leaves
  2. Wait for it to boil and when it starts bubbling, add sugar to the solution
  3. Wait for the aromatic fragrance and then strain the tea leaves from the solution and enjoy!
