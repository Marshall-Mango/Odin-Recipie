Choclate Chip Cookie

Delicious Choclate chip cookies

To make chocolate chip cookies, start by creaming together softened butter with brown and white sugars until fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract, then stir in flour, baking soda, and salt to form a dough. Fold in plenty of chocolate chips, scoop the dough onto a baking sheet, and bake until the edges are golden and the centers are soft. Let them cool slightly before devouring—warm, gooey perfection in every bite.

    Ingredients required for making Cookies
  • All Purpose Flour
  • 100 grams Processed Cheese
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Choclate Chips
    Steps for making Cookies
  1. Mix the dough: Cream together butter and sugars, then add eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour, baking soda, salt, and chocolate chips.
  2. Shape and bake: Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10–12 minutes until golden around the edges.
  3. Cool and enjoy: Let cookies cool slightly on the sheet, then transfer to a rack. Enjoy warm or store for later!
